🌿 ABOUT OPTICROP

Empowering Agriculture Through Artificial Intelligence

Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine is a machine learning-based web application that recommends the most suitable crop based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions, helping farmers improve productivity, optimize resource utilization, and support sustainable agriculture.

OUR MISSION

Building Smarter Agriculture

Accurate Crop Prediction

Predict the most suitable crop using Machine Learning based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions.

Sustainable Agriculture

Encourage eco-friendly farming practices that improve productivity while conserving natural resources.

Support Farmers

Empower farmers with AI-powered recommendations for smarter, faster, and more informed crop selection.

HOW IT WORKS

AI Crop Recommendation Process

OptiCrop AI follows a simple four-step process to recommend the best crop based on environmental and soil conditions.

Enter Soil Data

Provide Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, Temperature, Humidity, pH and Rainfall.

Machine Learning

The trained model analyzes all parameters using Machine Learning.

Prediction

AI predicts the most suitable crop for the given conditions.

Better Farming

Farmers receive recommendations for improved productivity.
